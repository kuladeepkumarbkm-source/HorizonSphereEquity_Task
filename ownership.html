Ownership

NSBLPA Ownership program enables league fans and investors to own a part of a franchise, mirroring the best of pro sports ownership. Inspired by SalesBets, our transparent, community-led process is at the heart of the league structure.

  • Join franchise ownership groups
  • Participate in league votes and decisions
  • Connect with other league stakeholders
  • Opportunities for both fans and partners
